All you need to know to get you started:

Basic tools

To get you started you don't need much, just grab a yarn (possibly something pretty thick to start) and an hook, you'll find the hooks have numbers, pick one that reflects the suggestion on the yarn's label, or bigger. get scissors too. and you are ready to go!

Basic stitches

you need to know few basic stitches and once you master them and put them togheter you'll see how more complicated patters will look just as simple to you. To start you need to learn how to chain, single crohet (sc), doble crochet (dc), half-double chrochet (hdc), increse, decrese, and slip stitch
